About the Project

WPL Mini Project — Academic Year 2024

Online Grievance Redressal System for College

This web application digitises the process of lodging and resolving grievances within an educational institution. Students and staff can submit complaints, track their status in real time, and receive email notifications on updates. The grievance cell can manage, filter, and respond to all submitted complaints through a dedicated portal.

Built with Django, Bootstrap 4, and PostgreSQL. Technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, Django.
